LAPTOPS

MacBook Pro laptop computers and leather carrying cases custom etched with the Oregon "O" are available for student athletes.

COMPUTER LABS

The Jaqua Center has 54 computer stations, divided between group and silent study rooms. Six additional stations equipped with the latest creative software can be found in a graphics lab located on the 3rd floor.

STUDY AREAS

All 35 tutor rooms in the Jaqua Center are setup with wall-mounted monitors that can be easily connected to your computer and large dry erase boards. Communal study spaces like the library, athlete lounge, and classrooms are also available.

IT STAFF

The IT staff will help you through any complications you may have with technical equipment in the Jaqua Center. They will also be your first stop should you encounter any issues with your laptop.

Reader Board

Daktronics board lists athlete appointment times, tutor rooms, and tutors.

AUDITORIUM

This 114-person lecture hall has custom designed seating—each chair is upholstered in Ferrari leather and wired for power.

Frosh Hall

Study area designated specifically for freshman. Room for two students per carrel. Overhead combination lockers.

ROBIN'S NEST

Grab a cup of coffee or a quick bite while studying. Menu items are named for Oregon’s acclaimed athletic alumni.

Lounge

Relax between tutor sessions or take a break from homework in the student-athlete lounge.
