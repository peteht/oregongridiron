EXPLORE JAQUA CENTER

Welcome to the

Jaqua Center

The John E. Jaqua Academic Center was designed with performance in mind. Built exclusively for use by student athletes, the facilities and services housed here will no doubt be part of your off-the-field achievement both at Oregon and after. This is where you come to get headstrong.

Learn More
DEVELOPING YOUR TALENTS IS OURS

Balancing between playbook and textbook can be a challenge, but you'll have all the resources necessary to make it as easy as possible. The Jaqua Center employs tutors, learning specialists, academic advisors, sports psychologists, life skills coaches and others as part of the team dedicated to helping you manage your work and time off the field.

Ahead of the Curve

The Jaqua Center is setup to take care of anything that might slow down the academic process, allowing athletes to focus on what matters when it's time to work. Enter the gallery to learn about a few of the features that make this facility so functional.
